About Me

Hi! This is Pu Zeng, I’m a data scientist with three years of experience working in the pharmaceutical industry, specializing in clinical trial analytics. My work focuses on transforming complex patient data into actionable insights that inform drug efficacy, safety, and regulatory decisions. I have a strong background in statistical programming, machine learning, and data visualization, and I routinely collaborate with cross-functional teams including clinical researchers and regulatory affairs. My passion lies in leveraging data to improve patient outcomes and accelerate the development of innovative therapies. I’m committed to rigorous science, clear communication, and continuous learning in this dynamic and impactful field.

Personal Values

I value integrity, curiosity, and collaboration. In my work, I strive for accuracy and transparency, especially when handling sensitive clinical data. I believe that science serves people best when it is ethical, inclusive, and driven by a desire to make lives better. I’m passionate about continuous improvement and view each project as an opportunity to learn and grow. Building trust, being accountable, and advocating for evidence-based decisions are central to my approach.
